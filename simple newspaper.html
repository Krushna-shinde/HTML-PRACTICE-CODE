Massive fire breaks out at Guwahati Medical College, no casualties reported
A massive fire broke out at Guwahati Medical College and Hospital on Monday evening. According to officials of Guwahati Medical College and Hospital, the fire broke out in a room of the Central Clinical Laboratory on the first floor of the old building of the government-run hospital. The GMCH authorities immediately informed the fire service about the incident and four fire tenders were engaged to douse the flame.
